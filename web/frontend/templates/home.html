About Track Insights

Welcome to Track Insights, your go-to app for IHSAA track and field playoff performances and data insights. Our mission is to promote Indiana high school track and field, celebrate achievements, motivate athletes, and provide meaningful insights for athletes, coaches, and fans.

The inspiration for this app came from a single question posed by an athlete:

"I wonder how our 4x800 time would place in other Sectionals?"

Manually checking state-wide results is tedious and error-prone. This challenge inspired our first project: to automate this functionality and help athletes quickly and easily see how their performances stack up in other Sectionals across the state. From there, many more ideas emerged, leading to the creation of additional tools designed to inform, motivate, and promote Indiana high school track and field.

Track Insights is a student-led initiative, developed by Park Tudor Computer Science students, and this app serves as a platform to share our work with you.

We hope you enjoy using Track Insights!

History

2023 – 2024

Created our first program that shows how athlete results would place in other Sectionals across the state. Shortly thereafter, we built a tool to generate result percentiles (e.g., 50th percentile for all events based on all Sectional performances), which was used at Park Tudor to help set fair Varsity Letter standards for each event.

2024 – 2025

Cal B, Malcolm H, Aashiv J, Justin L, Daniel L, Emily W, Owen W, Allison X: Designed and created the database and developed multiple data analysis tools. Results were posted on Instagram: @trackinsights

2025 – 2026

Arnay G, Justin L, Owen Z: Designed and created the web application, enhanced existing analysis tools, and developed new tools.
